Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Overview

TypingTest is a static website with no backend server, no user accounts, and no database. This policy explains exactly what data is stored when you use the site, and — just as importantly — what is not collected at all.

What Data Is Stored

TypingTest uses your browser's localStorage to save the following, entirely on your own device:

📈

Typing History

WPM, CPM, accuracy, and test details from tests you complete.

🎨

Theme Preference

Your chosen color theme (dark, light, blue, green, or high-contrast).

🔊

Sound Preference

Whether keystroke sound effects are enabled or muted.

🏆

Personal Bests

Your best scores per language and mode, used for the leaderboard view.

None of this information is transmitted to us, to any server, or to any third party. It exists solely in your browser and is only accessible from the device and browser you used to create it.

No Tracking or Analytics

TypingTest does not use cookies for tracking, does not run analytics scripts, and does not use advertising trackers of any kind. We do not know who you are, where you're located, or how you use the site beyond what happens locally in your own browser.

No Third-Party Sharing

Because no personal data is ever collected or transmitted by TypingTest, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or disclose to third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media trackers, or external analytics providers.

Your Right to Clear Your Data

Since all data lives in your browser, you are always in full control of it. You can clear your TypingTest data at any time by:

  • Using the Reset Statistics button on the Stats page.
  • Clearing your browser's site data or cookies for this website through your browser settings.
  • Using private/incognito browsing mode, which discards localStorage when the session ends.

Children's Privacy

TypingTest does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, because it collects no personal information at all. The site is safe for typing practice at any age.

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices ever change, we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our Contact page.
