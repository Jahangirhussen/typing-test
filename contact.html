Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a reply?

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days, though it may take longer during busy periods.

Can I report a bug through this form?

Yes — please include your browser, device, and steps to reproduce the issue so we can investigate quickly.

No, TypingTest has no account system. All support happens by email through this contact form.
