Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

We Don't Use Tracking Cookies

TypingTest does not set cookies to track you across the web, build an advertising profile, or monitor your browsing activity. There are no third-party cookies, no ad-tech cookies, and no analytics cookies anywhere on this site.

What We Actually Use: localStorage

Instead of cookies, TypingTest uses your browser's localStorage — a storage mechanism built into modern browsers that keeps information on your own device, not on any server. We use it to remember:

🎨

Theme Preference

Your selected color theme, so it's applied automatically on your next visit.

🔊

Sound Preference

Whether keystroke sounds are turned on or off.

📊

Typing History & Stats

Your past test results, personal bests, and progress over time.

This data is never transmitted to a server — it stays entirely within your browser and is only readable by TypingTest when you visit the site.

How to Clear This Data

You can remove locally stored data at any time by:

  • Clicking Reset Statistics on the Stats page.
  • Clearing your browser's cookies and site data for this website through your browser's settings menu.
  • Browsing in private or incognito mode, which does not persist localStorage between sessions.

Changes to This Policy

Should our use of local storage ever change, we will update this page accordingly. We recommend checking back periodically.
