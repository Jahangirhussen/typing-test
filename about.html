About TypingTest

A free, no-signup typing speed test for English and Bangla, built for anyone who wants to type faster and more accurately.

About the Website

TypingTest is a free online typing practice platform that helps you measure and improve your typing speed and accuracy in both English and Bangla. Whether you're a student preparing for a typing exam, a professional who spends hours at the keyboard, or simply someone who wants to type without looking down, TypingTest gives you real-word datasets, multiple difficulty levels, and instant feedback on every test.

There's no account to create and no paywall to hit. Open the site, choose a language and mode, and start typing. Your results — words per minute (WPM), characters per minute (CPM), and accuracy — are calculated the moment you finish, and your history is saved right in your own browser so you can track your progress over time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality typing practice accessible to everyone, regardless of language, device, or budget. We believe a fast, accurate typist has a real edge — in school, at work, and online — and that practicing shouldn't require downloads, subscriptions, or handing over personal data. TypingTest exists to remove every barrier between you and consistent, measurable typing practice.

Our Vision

We envision TypingTest as the go-to typing companion for English and Bangla speakers alike — a place where a student in Dhaka and a professional anywhere else can practice with equally high-quality, natural text, get the same detailed feedback, and grow their skills at their own pace. As we grow, we want to keep expanding our lesson library, word datasets, and tools while staying true to our core promise: simple, fast, and free.

Technology

TypingTest is built as a lightweight, static website using plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step, no backend server. This keeps the site fast, easy to host, and simple to inspect. All of your typing history, statistics, and preferences (like theme and sound settings) are stored using your browser's localStorage, which means your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself.

Because there's no database or account system, TypingTest can run entirely from static files, load quickly on any connection, and work reliably without tracking scripts or third-party analytics slowing things down.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TypingTest really free?

Yes. Every feature — tests, lessons, stats, and certificates — is completely free with no sign-up required.

Do I need to create an account?

No. TypingTest works entirely without accounts. Your progress is saved locally in your browser.

What happens to my typing data?

All of your test history and statistics are stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to or stored on any server.

Can I use TypingTest on mobile?

Yes, the site is fully responsive, though a physical keyboard gives the most accurate typing test experience.

Who maintains TypingTest?

TypingTest is an independent project maintained by a small team passionate about typing education and accessible web tools.
