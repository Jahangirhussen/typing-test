FAQ

How is WPM calculated?

WPM = (correct characters typed / 5) divided by minutes elapsed — the standard "5 characters = 1 word" convention.

Does the text repeat?

No — a no-repeat system avoids showing recently used sentences/paragraphs until the pool is exhausted.

Can I change difficulty mid-test?

Yes, changing any control instantly restarts the test with fresh content.
